Langdedrosa Cyanwrath is a member of the Dragon Cult and one of its Champions. He participated in the Blue Dragon Attack, and broke the agreement that Severin the Red made with the town of Greenest to leave without any more deaths after they surrendered their treasury and the Book of Dragon Rituals to the cult. After the rest of the Cult had left, Langdedrosa arrived at the town's keep and issued a 'challenge' to the town's greatest warrior, in exchange for the release of his four prisoners, the sister of a Greenest guardsman named Markguth, as well as her three children. Unwilling to let any more innocents die, and filled with rage after the death of his father, Valen, Balrick Stonefist rose to the challenge and was quickly defeated by the Half-Dragon. In disgust, Langdedrosa spared Balrick from a final blow and demanded he come find his when he was worthy of a quick death.
