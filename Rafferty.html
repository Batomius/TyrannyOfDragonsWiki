Rafferty, often called Uncle Rafferty by Daisy and her companions, is a Greenest Halfling and traitor to the dragon cult. He inadvertently brought the events of the Blue Dragon Attack to Greenest when he fled the cult and stole a Book of Dragon Rituals, which he had been helping translate for Severin the Red. After joining the cult, Rafferty had eventually realized that he was helping to release a great evil on the world, and began to delay his research findings, however, when Severin brought in foreign wizards to 'help' with his research, he realized that his ruse could go on no longer. He stole the book and fled to Greenest, where the cult soon followed him, leading to the attack.

In an attempt to spare the citizens of Greenest, Rafferty agreed to return to the cult with the book in hand, with his life being guaranteed by the cults leader, Severin. He said goodbye to his niece, Daisy, and walked back to be a prisoner once more.
