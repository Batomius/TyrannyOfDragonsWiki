Summary

Kaboom! is a red Kobold that the party met during their return to the Dragon Cult Camp. He was either exiled or defected (its unclear) from the cult, and now considers them his enemy. He helped Daisy escape the caves, and continued to journey with the party thereafter.


Bombs

Kaboom is an alchemist, and he specializes in all the explosive aspects of that craft. he has an Arsenal of different weapons he can create, but requires resources to create them.
