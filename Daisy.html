Daisy is a Young female Halfling from the village of Greenest. Before the Attack of the Blue Dragon, she had been traveling to [Berdusk] in search of some Berdusken Tobacco for her uncle. On the road there, she stopped at an inn called between Greenest and Berdusk called The Greengrove Inn, where she met Batomius Plunk and Balrick Stonefist, as well as a merchant who sold her some Berdusken Tobacco. Having achieved her goal, she agreed to accompany her new companions back to Greenest and thus witnessed the dragon attack.
