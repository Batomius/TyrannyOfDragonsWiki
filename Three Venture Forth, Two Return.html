Three Venture Forth, Two Return







Just as the direness of their situation became crystal clear, the scene under the caves was interrupted. A strange, cackling shape arrived with a bang and demanded that any "enemies of the cult, come with me and live, or stay and die". Daisy rushed to the newcomer's side, desperate for any chance of survival, but Batomius moved too slowly from where he had lain on the floor, dying. Langdedrosa and his remaining henchman had already gotten between them, and he decided to flee down another tunnel instead. He glanced back, first at Daisy and her fleeing companion, then at Balrick, who lay unmoving upon the floor.

Daisy followed the mysterious creature through the tunnels, avoiding traps at his indication, and the other dangers of the caves, as well. Quicker than she expected, they had reached a place of safety, and her new companion revealed himself. He turned out to be a red Kobold, named Kaboom, who had turned enemy of the cult when they discouraged his alchemical ambitions.


Kaboom!



Daisy performed a ritual she had been left by her Uncle, which summoned a [Brass Wyrmling] to be her companion. Kaboom, wary of metallic dragons, initially wanted to kill it, but agreed not to upon Daisy's request.

With the help of Kaboom, Daisy lures a Horrifying Monster back to the chamber where Balrick was killed. it engaged in combat with Langdedrosa and his lackeys, while Daisy and Kaboom made their escape.



Some of Kaboom!'s Creations



Meanwhile, Batomius managed to escape the winding tunnels to emerge at the cave entrance, and waited patiently for his companion to emerge.

Once reunited, Daisy, introduces Batomius to her two new companions, and they start the long journey back to Greenest, a Balrick-shaped hole in their midst.
