Greenest

Greenest is the home town of Balrick Stonefist and Daisy, amongst others. It is the largest village in the immediate rejion, and is famous locally for its Harvest Festival.

It was also the sight of a Blue Dragon Attack, which caused considerable damage and loss of life.
