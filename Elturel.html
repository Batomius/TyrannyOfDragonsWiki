Elturel

Elturel is a large bustling city, and capital of the country of Elturgard, in the Western Heartlands.

It's most prominent feature is the second sun that floats above it at all hours of the day. It is said that a miracle brought the sun to the city, and saved them from certain disaster.


Hellriders

A Hellrider is a soldier in service to the city of Elturel. They are not part of the Order of the Companion, but many harbor hopes of joining their ranks.


Second Sun

Elturel is famous for the large sun that hangs over the city at all times of the day.
