A Road Seldom Trodden






Upon their return to Greenest, Daisy and Batomius made their way to see Tarbaw Nighthill and report their progress on uncovering the cults plans. As a reward for their work, Nighthill gave them a letter and told them to deliver it to Auntie Rosylnn, who would give them their reward.

While on their way to return to Daisy's home, where they had left Kaboom to rest, they ran into a strange silver Dragonborn, by the name of Suor Lyth. This strange character claimed to be another follower of Bahamut, and also claimed to have been sent to find them and fight the cult with them. He was to travel to Elturel, which gave the party pause, as they had not shared their destination with anyone.

After some deliberation, they decided to journey together, and set of to visit Auntie Rosylnn, before setting off for Elturel.

Reunited with Nesim, and then Kaboom!, the party set off. Upon reaching Rosylnn's farm, they were treated to baked goods and blueberry tea, before eventually having their reward revealed. Rosylnn took them to her fields and revealed dozens of horses, any of which they could take as they pleased.

Daisy found herself a monstrous black draft horse, while Batomius took a rangy and aging gelding, that looked as though it might die before they reached Elturel. With that, they party left Rosylnn, and Greenest, behind.

It was six days travel to Elturel, and, other than an encounter with a [Manticore], the travel was uneventful.

On the sixth day, they found themselves standing outside the walls of Elturel, the next step in their adventure before them.
