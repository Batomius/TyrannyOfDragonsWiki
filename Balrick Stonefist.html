Balrick Stonefist is a Dwarven Palidan from the village of Greenest, in the Green Feilds region of Central Fearun. He has devoted his life to the god [Bahamut] and spent the last 5 years serving as a missionary in his name before finally being anointed as a paladin. On his journey back to his home, he stopped for the night at The Greengrove Inn, where he met Batomius Plunk and Daisy. The three soon decided to journey together, and left the next morning for Greenest.

Upon returning to his home, he found it overrun by kobolds and cultists, while a blue dragon circled in the skies above.
