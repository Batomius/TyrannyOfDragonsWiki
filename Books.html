Books

This Page contains a list of books seen, read, or otherwise obtained by Batomius Plunk and his companions. In most cases, a brief description is given, while those of particular interest will have their own pages.

On the Lesser Races, by Claus Gormavinche - A non-fiction book about the races of Faerun of diminutive stature. Despite Batomius' belief, the book is riddled with racist undertones, and much of the work is exaggeration when not entirely fiction. Despite this, it does contain some actual insight into the lives and histories of Gnomes, Halflings, and Dwarves.


Book of Dragon Rituals - The Book of Dragon Rituals is a mysterious text that contains numerous powerful and dangerous spells in an unknown language, all of which revolve around dragons. It is a key part of the Dragon Cult's plans.
