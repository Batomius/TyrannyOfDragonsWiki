Goblin Attacks in Greenest (Complete)

The leaflet about the goblin raids

    At The Greengrove Inn, Batomius took a leaflet from the notice board asking for a party of adventurers to investigate goblin attacks in Greenest.

    After the events of the Blue Dragon Attack, it became obvious that these attacks were in fact carried out by the Dragon Cult's Kobolds.
