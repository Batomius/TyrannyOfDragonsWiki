Meet Leosin in Elturel (Complete)

While the party returned to the Dragon Cult Camp to recover their lost possessions, Leosin stayed in bed to recover. He indicated that he would soon leave for Elturel, and asked the party to come and meet him upon the completion of their business in Greenest. Nesim offered to stay in Greenest, and accompany them to Elturel on their return.

After returning for the camp, the party set off with Nesim towards Elturel.

After reaching the city, the party journeyed to A Pair of Black Antlers Tavern, and met Ontharr Frume. Leosin told the party that they needed to Gain Ontharr's Trust in order for him to join the fight against the Dragon Cult.
