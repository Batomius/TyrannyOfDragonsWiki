Gain Ontharr's Trust

After meeting Leosin at A Pair of Black Antlers in Elturel, the party was introduced to Ontharr Frume, the leader of the Order of the Gauntlet. Leosin advised the party that he would be a valuable ally against the Dragon Cult, and asked them to gain his trust.
