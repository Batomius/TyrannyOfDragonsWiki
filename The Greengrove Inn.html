The Greengrove Inn

The Greengrove Inn is an inn that stands on the road between Greenest and Berdusk.

It is run by Hubert Alden, the Innkeeper, and his twin brother, Galter Alden, who works as a Hostler.

It is the location that Batomius, Balrick, and Daisy first met, as well as where they first met [], and fist layed eyes on Sir Isteval, Unknown Cormyrean War Wizard and the [unknown half-elf].

While at the Inn, the party received a note detailing Goblin Attacks in Greenest.
