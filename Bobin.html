Summary

Bobin is friend of Daisy's from Greenest. He is mute and communicates by sign.


Alter Ego

Bobin is also known as the Potato Bandit, the scourge of Greenest. With his cunning disguise, he and his band of Ne'er-do-wells terrorise the farms around Greenest by stealing their potatoes.
