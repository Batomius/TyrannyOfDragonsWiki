Summary

Leosin traveled through the Greengrove Inn on his way to Greenest, seemingly on his way to join his fellow cultists. He was spotted by the party to be in the possession of a mysterious Silver Dragon Mask, and his appearance gave considerable agitation to Severin Silrajin. At that time, the party did not know Severin's true identity and believed Leosin to be a true cultist. Upon reaching Greenest and the events that followed, they soon learned that he had been attempting to infiltrate the cult and they had unwittingly contributed to his discovery.
