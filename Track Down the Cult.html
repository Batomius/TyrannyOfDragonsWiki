Track Down The Cult

The Governor of Greenest, Tarbaw Nighthill, requested the party's aide and asked them to Track Down the Dragon Cult. He bade them to return with information about their numbers and plans, as well as word of any prisoners they still had with them. He also asked them to bring word of the treasures that were taken as a condition of the truce after the Blue Dragon Attack.
