Background

Batomius woke up in a burned-down ruin with no memory of who or where he was. His only hints were the few clues he found since waking. One of those things was a mostly burned spell book, only six pages and their spells remaining, as well as half a page with a scribbled and barely legible name that he took for his own.

He feels a pull of curiosity towards almost everything. He isn't sure if this is a result of his new perspective or if it is part of the old him leaking through. Whatever the cause, Batomius becomes obsessed with the pursuit of knowledge and often makes reckless decisions to achieve his goals. He is also often tactless in conversation but without malice. These flaws are at odds with his generally decent nature and his belief that people are generally a force for good.
