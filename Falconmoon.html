Falconmoon is a Priest of Chauntea stationed in Greenest. During the Blue Dragon Attack, he barricaded his temple in an attempt to protect the worshipers there from the enemy. Batomius Plunk, Daisy and Balrick Stonefist attempted to help Falconmoon and his people to escape, but they arrived too late, and the temple was quickly overtaken.

Falconmoon survived the attack and continues to serve as a priest in Greenest.
