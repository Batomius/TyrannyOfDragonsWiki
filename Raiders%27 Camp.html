Raiders' Camp

The Raiders' Camp, also called the Dragon Cult Camp, was an encampment of Dragon Cultists in the Greenfields region of the Western Heartlands. From this camp, the Blue Dragon Attack on Greenest was launched, and the loot from this and other attacks like it was stored here until it was taken north to Naerytar.

Batomius Plunk, Balrick Stonefist, and Daisy were captured sneaking into this camp while trying to uncover the cults plans. Here, they also found Leosin Erlanthar and Farmer Richard, and escaped with them.

After escorting their companions to safety, the party returned to the camp and searched the caves they surrounded. They managed to recover their stolen possessions, but Balrick Stonefist died at the hands of Langdedrosa Cyanwrath.

This was also the location that the party met Kaboom! and Hank.
