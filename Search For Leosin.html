Search For Leosin (Complete)


In Greenest, the party were approached by Nesim Waladra, the apprentice of Leosin. He asked for any word of his master, and the party told him the last place they had seen him was when he had been captured by Severin the Red. He then asked if the party would search For Leosin on their travels, and they confirmed that they would.

The party found Leosin when they were captured at the Dragon Cult Camp, and managed to rescue him as they escaped themselves.

They returned an injured Leosin to Nesim in Greenest, where he stayed to recover from his wounds.
