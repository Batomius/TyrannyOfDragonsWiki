Background

Suor Lyth is a Silver Dragonborn Paladin from the village of Greenest, in the Green Fields region of Central Fearun. He has devoted his life to the god Bahamut. He returned to Greenest after the destruction of his temple, and the deaths of first Valen Stormbringer, and then Balrick Stonefist.
