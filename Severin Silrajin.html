Summary

Severin, often known as Severin the Red, is the leader of the Dragon Cult and sworn enemy of the adventurers Daisy, Batomius Plunk, and Balrick Stonefist. He was instrumental in co-ordinating the Blue Dragon Attack which devastated the town of Greenest and killed many of its citizens.

The party first met Severin at the Greengrove Inn the day before the dragon attack. Despite his initial unwillingness to discuss his past in detail, it soon became clear that Severin knew a lot about the Dragon Cult and had seemingly fought them previously. It seemed to the party that they had met a great warrior, and they soon grew to respect Severin for his keen intelligence and sharp eyes. After some more arrivals at the Inn, Severin grew tense and said he would return to Greenest the next day, if not sooner.

As the party slept that evening, Severin left the inn, leaving them a note instructing them to follow and find him when they could. By the time the party reached the town, however, the attack had already begun.

Only after the party had fallen at the Temple of Chauntea did they reunited with Severin, but their reunion was an unexpected one. They awoke as prisoners, and a man in a red dragon mask, obviously the leader of the group holding them captive, entered the room. He then revealed himself to be Severin and asked for the parties help, and even invited them to his organization. The party refused his invitation, but agreed to help him end the conflict with no further loss of life.

After receiving the treasury of Greenest, along with the Book of Dragon Rituals and the cult defector Uncle Rafferty, Severin kept his word and left the town without further violence. But for Balrick, Daisy, and Batomius, the damage had already been done. They knew their fates would take them back to Severin, and either they would die, or he would.
