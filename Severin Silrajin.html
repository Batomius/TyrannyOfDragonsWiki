Summary

Severin is a mysterious figure the party met at the Greengrove Inn. Despite his unwillingness to discuss his past in detail, it soon became clear that Severin knew a lot about the [Dragon Cult] who would, only the next day, attack Greenest. As the party slept that evening, Severin left the inn, leaving them a note instructing them to follow and find him when they could. By the time the party reached the town, however, the attack had already begun.
