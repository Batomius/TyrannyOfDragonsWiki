The Greenfields Takings
    A stack of papers taken from Frulam Mondath's study in the cave at the [Raiders Camp].

    They form a ledger listing the items looted from the [Greenfields] Region, where they were stolen from, their expected value, and their destination. Aside from a few items left blank, every row reads "Naerytar" in the destination column.
